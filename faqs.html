Questions and more questions

Why to pick us?

We are an option that offer you quality products, with us you can find the delicious traditional baking that we all love and also the new tendencies in techniques.

How do I put an order?

You just need to contact us thought our page! You can pick something from our product range, or if you have a special idea or request let us know in the message and we will get back to you shortly to discuss the idea and give you the best option to achieve it.

Times

In the case of traditional baking, orders need to be put in at least 2 days prior to the delivery date.
For birthday cakes, we need a week to prepare it, depending on the design choosen. Since some of them could require special materials that need to be ordered.
